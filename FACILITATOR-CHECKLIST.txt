CAPGEMINI BUILD CHALLENGE - FACILITATOR CHECKLIST
=================================================

BEFORE THE ROOM
1. Open START-HERE.txt.
2. Confirm the Capgemini blank canvas opens.
3. Ask Claude to check the app is ready.

DURING THE ROOM
1. Ask the participant what they want to build.
2. Let Claude build the first usable version fast.
3. After each change, ask the participant to try it.
4. Keep the idea small enough to finish in 2 hours.

IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG
1. Tell Claude what happened in plain words.
2. Let Claude fix and check it.
3. Continue only when the participant can try it again.
